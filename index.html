What do we want? More BPD body cameras

Our group has choosen police brutality to focus on, and to try to reduce. We believe that the BPD body camera program is an excellent way to reduce police violence, and add accountablility back into our justice system. We feel passionate about this issue because it impacts our family, friends, and community as a whole. Police violence has gotten to the point where most of our classmates have witnessed their own family members shot and killed. If you want to get involved and help prevent this problem, you can use this website to speak out. In the Speak Out section below you can contact your local representative, via the "Find your Rep" form or you can contact the Mayor of Boston, or the Boston Police Comissioner.
